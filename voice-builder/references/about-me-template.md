# about-me.md Template

Produce a compact identity file that other agents can load before writing for this profile. Maximum: 300 words.

Use only interview answers. Do not add voice rules here.

```markdown
# About Me · {Profile Name}

> Slug: `{profile-slug}` · Built on {YYYY-MM-DD} with voice-builder.

## Who

{Name or brand, role, project, what they sell, communicate, or represent. 1 to 2 concrete sentences.}

## Context and Maturity

{Years active, team size, business size, audience size, stage, or relevant operating context. If not confirmed, write "tamaño no confirmado" or "contexto no declarado".}

## Ideal Audience

{1 to 2 concrete sentences: who this voice speaks to, their stage, sector, problem, or desired outcome.}

## Not the Audience

{Explicit filters. Who should not feel addressed by this voice.}

## Core Topics

1. {Topic 1}. POV: {specific point of view, or "POV no declarado, extraer de muestras"}.
2. {Topic 2}. POV: {specific point of view, or "POV no declarado, extraer de muestras"}.
3. {Topic 3}. POV: {specific point of view, or "POV no declarado, extraer de muestras"}.
4. {Topic 4}. POV: {specific point of view, or "POV no declarado, extraer de muestras"}.
5. {Topic 5 if relevant}. POV: {specific point of view, or "POV no declarado, extraer de muestras"}.
6. {Topic 6 if relevant}. POV: {specific point of view, or "POV no declarado, extraer de muestras"}.

## Core Belief

{The counterintuitive belief or editorial thesis that connects the profile's communication. 1 to 2 sentences.}

## Anti-Topics

{Topics the profile does not cover. One line or compact list.}

## Brand Promise

{What the audience should associate with this person or brand. 1 sentence.}

## Secondary Audiences

{Talent, partners, investors, team, community, students, or "none declared". Include whether voice changes for them.}

## Additional Notes

{Any context that affects generation but does not belong above.}
```

## Fill Rules

- Keep every line usable by another agent.
- Prefer concrete nouns over adjectives.
- Do not invent facts that were not stated in the interview.
- If a section has no answer, write `no declarado en entrevista`.
- Do not include confidential details unless the user clearly wants them stored in the profile.
- Do not include writing-style rules here. Put those in `voice.md`.
